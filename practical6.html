Dr.BR AMBEDKAR - The Father of Modern India

Dr.Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was a visionary leader,social reformer, and the architect of the Indian constitution.

Early Life

He was born in Mhow, MP into a Dalit family. He faced severe discrimination and social injustice in his early years.

Education

B.A in Economics and Political Science from Bombay University. M.A, Ph.d, D.Sc from london School of Economics.

Achievements

  • Served as chairman of drafting comittee of India.
  • Advocate for liberty, equality and fraternity.
  • Ensured Fundamental rights and protection against discrimination.
